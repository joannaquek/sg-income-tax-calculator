Top-up simulator

Charts plot estimated net tax payable on the horizontal axis and top-up amount on the vertical axis, so each point shows the pair (tax outcome, contribution level). Applies on top of what you entered on the Reliefs step — scroll charts sideways on small screens.

The top-up simulator is most useful for tax residents. Non-residents do not receive the same personal reliefs — treat this chart as illustrative only.

Reliefs (SRS, CPF top-up, life insurance) count toward the $80,000 personal income tax relief cap per year. Donations to approved IPCs use a 250% tax deduction and are separate from that cap. Annual caps: SRS S$15,300 (SC/SPR) or S$35,700 (foreigner); CPF cash top-up S$8,000 (self) and S$8,000 (family) per calendar year — check IRAS for your situation.
